// Teacher app features

Every tool solves
a real problem.

Nothing in Layers is there for the sake of it. Each feature was built around something that actually interrupts lessons and presentations.

⏱

Timer

Set a countdown without leaving your slide. Choose from quick presets — 30 seconds, 1, 2, 3, 5, or 10 minutes — or type any time you want. A buzzer sounds when it ends so you don't have to watch it.

✏

Screen Drawing & Annotation

Draw, circle, underline, or highlight directly on top of anything on your screen. Point things out clearly without switching apps. Pen, highlighter, arrows, shapes — undo any stroke instantly.

⚄

Random Student Picker

End the awkward silence of "who wants to answer?" A fun spinning display picks a student at random. Add your class list once — names, numbers, or both — and use it every lesson.

⚠

Noise Monitor

A live display shows how loud the room is — green when calm, yellow when rising, red when it's too much. Set the level that's right for your class and an alert sounds automatically when it's exceeded.

🎙

Live Speech Translation

Hold a button, speak normally, and your words are translated in real time into 46 languages. A large, floating caption window shows the translation so every student in the room can read along.

🌐

Focused Browser

Open a clean, minimal browser window — no address bar, no personal tabs, no distractions — for quick lookups during your lesson. Students only see what you're looking up, nothing else.

🔒

PIN Lock

Set a four-digit code so students cannot disable Focus Mode or disconnect from your classroom without your permission. Gives you real authority over their screens from where you're standing.

🚫

Site Blocking

If a website is causing problems, block it for the whole class instantly. It vanishes from every student's screen the moment you add it — no need to touch a single device.

📂

Quick File Launcher

Open any document, PDF, spreadsheet, or presentation directly from the toolbar. No more hunting through File Explorer mid-lesson — your materials open in one click from wherever Layers is.
