Step 03

Pair your devices

Connect the child's device to your parent dashboard with the pairing code.

⏱️ About 1 minute
1

Find your pairing code

Use the code you wrote down in Step 1 when setting up the child's device.

2

Enter the code on your parent device

In the Guard Controller app or Web Dashboard, tap "Enter Pairing Code" and type the code. The devices connect within seconds.

✅ Once paired, the connection is permanent. You don't need to pair again unless you reset the child's device.
🔗
Devices are connected! Your dashboard now shows the child's online status, active apps, and screen time.
