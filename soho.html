// How it works

Ready before your
client sits down.

Open your laptop, choose your languages, turn the screen to face them. That's it. You're ready. They don't install anything. They don't need to touch their phone.

01
🖥️

Set up in 10 seconds — before they sit down

Place your laptop on the table between you. Their translation appears at the top of the screen — facing them, in their language, large enough to read from across a table. Yours is at the bottom. No configuration. Nothing for them to set up.

02
🎙️

Speak naturally. They understand immediately.

Hold the talk button while you speak — just like a walkie-talkie. Release it. Within seconds, your words appear in your client's language on their side of the screen. No waiting, no copy-pasting, no lost eye contact while you fumble with a phone.

03
🤝

They reply. You read it. The deal moves forward.

They hold the button on their side and reply in their own language. You read the translation instantly. Back and forth — natural, unhurried, professional. No phones on the table. No awkward silences. Just a conversation that actually goes somewhere.

// Built for business meetings

Everything you need
to walk out with a yes.

Every tool in Layers Talk SOHO was put there because of a specific moment in a business meeting that used to go wrong. These are the moments it fixes.

🎙️

Your client reads your offer before you've finished saying it

Speak normally. Within seconds, your words appear on their side of the screen — in their language, large enough to read from across the table. Price, terms, timeline — understood clearly, the first time. No re-explaining. No misread expressions. 47 languages, so whoever you're meeting, you're covered.

Core feature
🖥️

They see a professional setup before you say a word

The moment you open the laptop, their language is already waiting for them at the top of the screen. No fumbling with settings in front of the client. No "hold on, let me set this up." You walk in looking ready — and that confidence sets the tone for everything that follows.

✏️

Circle the exact clause you're agreeing to — before doubt creeps in

When language is imperfect, precision matters even more. Draw a circle around the price. Draw an arrow to the delivery date. Highlight the clause you're both agreeing to. When both sides can see exactly what's being discussed — visually, unambiguously — misunderstandings become almost impossible.

🧮

Work out the discount together — visibly, so there's no dispute later

They counter your price. You pull up the calculator right on screen — they can see the numbers — and work through the margin together. The transparency builds trust. The result is visible to both of you. Nobody leaves the meeting wondering if they misunderstood a figure.

📂

Show your product catalogue without breaking the flow of the conversation

One click opens your proposal, product sheet, or price list — sized to fit between both toolbars, visible from both sides of the table. No Alt-Tab. No minimising the translation. No rummaging through folders while they wait. Your materials appear exactly when the conversation calls for them.

⏱️

Keep the meeting moving without either side checking their phone

When you're both being polite and neither side wants to be the first to say "we should wrap up," a visible countdown does it for you — without the awkwardness. Set it at the start: "We have 20 minutes." Everyone stays on topic. The meeting ends on time and on a high note.

⌨️

When the room is quiet or the mic struggles — type it and move on

A noisy café. A product name with unusual pronunciation. A technical spec that the microphone keeps mangling. Just type it. Hit Enter. It translates exactly as you wrote it, precisely. The conversation doesn't stall — it keeps moving.

💾

A written record of everything agreed — before either of you leaves the room

Every word said, every translation, every moment of agreement — all logged with a timestamp. Before the client has reached the car park, you have a complete written record. Use it to write the follow-up email in minutes. Share it with your colleague who wasn't in the room. Or keep it on file in case a term is ever disputed.

New — CSV export
🌐

Show them a product page or a map — without exposing your whole screen

Pull up your product on the manufacturer's website. Show them the location on a map. Open a live exchange rate. A clean, minimal window opens between the toolbars — no address bar, no personal tabs visible, nothing to distract from the point you're making. Professional, contained, deliberate.
