Hi, I'm Peter.

I teach international students in Japan. My classroom has students who speak Vietnamese, Chinese, Nepali, Filipino, and more — many of them struggle to follow lessons in Japanese or English every single day.

I looked for a tool that could help. Something that would give students real-time translation, a dictionary, focus tools, and let me control their screens during class — all without leaving my slides. It didn't exist. So I built it.

That became Layers.

The Chrome extension is completely free — no ads, no data collection, no account required. Every student deserves access to tools that help them learn, regardless of what language they speak or how much their school can afford.

But building and maintaining Layers takes time, and I do it entirely on my own — before school, after school, on weekends. Every coffee you buy is a vote of confidence that says "keep going".

Here's what your support goes toward:

☕ Keeping Layers free and ad-free — forever. No student should see an ad when they're trying to learn.
🌍 Better translation and language support — more accurate, more languages, faster.
📚 New tools students actually need — built from real classroom feedback.
🔧 Server costs and maintenance — Firebase, hosting, the things that keep the sync features running.
💛 My motivation — honestly, knowing someone out there finds this useful means the world to me.

If Layers has helped you teach a class, understand a lesson, or just made your day a little easier — that's why it exists.

Thank you for being here.

— Peter
